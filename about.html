About Etherparty

As we move towards a Blockchain connected experience, we will increasingly rely on smart contracts to deliver automation. Etherparty was created to enable user defined contracts to ease the transition into this new economy.

When we think about what is a Blockchain, it is merely a record of transactions that have occurred across the network. In conjunction, Ethereum is a platform for distributed computing and at the core of this technology is conditionality that adds instructions to Blockchain transactions.

Bitcoin - is a peer to peer digital currency
Ethereum - is a distributed computing network
Blockchain - is a platform that is individually used by both of these technologies
Smart Contract - Computer protocol that executes the terms of a contract. Also known as an “Executable Decentralized Contract”

Etherparty combines all of them into a singular experience.
